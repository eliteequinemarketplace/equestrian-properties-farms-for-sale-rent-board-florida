Elite Equine Marketplace connects buyers, sellers, and renters of Florida equestrian properties and barns — covering the two most important hunter jumper markets in North America: Wellington and Ocala. Florida is the undisputed winter capital of the hunter jumper world, and the demand for equestrian property in Wellington and Ocala is unlike anywhere else on earth during the January through April winter circuit season.

Wellington, Florida — The Most Competitive Equestrian Rental Market in the World

The demand for equestrian properties near Wellington's Winter Equestrian Festival is extraordinary — and the rental market reflects it. Seasonal farm rentals in Wellington during the WEF season command some of the highest equestrian property rental rates in the world, with full farm rentals ranging from several thousand to tens of thousands of dollars per month depending on facility quality, stall count, and proximity to PBIEC. Elite Equine Marketplace lists Wellington seasonal farm rentals, barn leases, and short-term property arrangements directly from property owners — connecting riders, trainers, and barn managers with the right Wellington facility for their program.

Ocala, Florida — Year-Round Equestrian Real Estate

Ocala and Marion County are home to one of the largest concentrations of horse farms in the world — a year-round equestrian real estate market with significant activity across purchase, long-term lease, and seasonal rental categories. The opening of World Equestrian Center in 2021 dramatically increased demand for properties near the WEC campus, and seasonal farm rentals for the WEC Winter Spectacular (December–March) are among the most sought-after in the market. Elite Equine Marketplace lists Ocala horse farms for sale, barn leases, and seasonal rentals from verified Florida property owners and managers.
